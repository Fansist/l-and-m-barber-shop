Our Shop

A Clairemont Fixture for Sharp, Honest Cuts

L&M Barber Shop is a local Clairemont barbershop known for skin fades, beard shaves with a razor, and carved hair designs. It's the kind of shop where you drop in, get in the chair, and walk out looking right, no appointment required.

Simple, honest pricing and a relaxed, welcoming vibe have earned the shop 4.9 stars and 30 reviews on Birdeye, with regulars praising the precise fade work.
